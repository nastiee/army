What it is K-pop?

K-pop is a music genre that encompasses many styles of South Korean pop music. the preview page Including various areas of dance music, electropop, hip-hop and modern rhythm and blues.

The most popular K-pop group

There are many popular groups in Korea, and the most popular are groups such as BTS, BLACKPINK, EXO and other.

BLACKPINK

South Korean women's music group from Seoul, performing music in the genres of K-pop, hip-hop, dance-pop, teen-pop. Founded in 2016 by YG Entertainment. Participants: Kim Jisu, Kim Jenny, Pak Cheon (Rose) and Lalisa Manoban (Lisa).

BTS

BTS-a South Korean band founded in 2013. It consists of seven participants: RM, Jin, Shuga, J-Hope, Chimin, V, Chonguk.The band members are involved in writing music and lyrics for most of their songs.The lyrics of BTS songs often focus the listener's attention on personal and social issues, the difficulties of school-age youth, the path a person must take to accept and love themselves, and individualism.
